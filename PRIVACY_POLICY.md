# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 10, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

VideoSummary ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

### 2.1 Account Information
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Password (encrypted)
- Account preferences

### 2.2 Usage Data
- YouTube video URLs you submit for summarization
- Channels you subscribe to within our service

- Payment information is processed by our payment provider (Stripe/PayPal)

- Error logs and diagnostic data

We use your information to:

### 3.1 Provide the Service
- Generate AI summaries of YouTube videos
- Manage your account and subscriptions
- Process payments
- Send service-related notifications

### 3.2 Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns
- Fix bugs and improve performance
- Develop new features
- Train and improve our AI models

### 3.3 Communication
- Send account-related emails
- Respond to support requests
- Send marketing emails (with your consent)
- Notify you of changes to our service

### 3.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect against fraud and abuse

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: When you agree to receive marketing emails
- **Contract**: To provide the Service you signed up for
- **Legitimate Interest**: To improve our service and prevent fraud
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with laws and regulations

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:
